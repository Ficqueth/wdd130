History

In the heart of adventure and the rush of untamed rivers, White Water Rafting was born from a passion for exploration and the thrill of conquering nature's wildest waterways. Established in 1995 by a group of avid outdoors enthusiasts, our founders envisioned a company that would redefine the way people experienced the power and beauty of rivers. The journey began with a modest fleet of rafts and a small team of seasoned guides who shared a common dream: to introduce the world to the exhilarating world of white water rafting. Our first expeditions took place on the legendary Cascade River, where the rapids tested the mettle of every adventurer. From the very beginning, safety and excitement were at the forefront of our mission. As the reputation of White Water Rafting grew, so did our expeditions. We expanded our operations to include a variety of rivers across the country, each offering a unique blend of challenges and breathtaking scenery. From the roaring torrents of the Thunder Gorge to the meandering beauty of the Serene Valley River, our rafting experiences became synonymous with unforgettable moments and adrenaline-pumping thrills.
In the early 2000s, we pioneered the concept of eco-friendly rafting, championing responsible tourism and conservation efforts along the rivers we navigated. Our commitment to preserving the natural beauty of the waterways became an integral part of the White Water Rafting ethos, and we continue to lead the industry in sustainable adventure tourism. Over the years, countless adventurers, families, and thrill-seekers have entrusted us with their quest for excitement, making White Water Rafting a household name in the world of outdoor recreation.
Today, with a diverse range of rafting packages, state-of-the-art equipment, and a team of expert guides, we invite you to join us on a journey through the rapids, creating memories that will last a lifetime.
